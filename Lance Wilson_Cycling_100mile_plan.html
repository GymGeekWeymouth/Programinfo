Main Set · Sweet Spot
M1
Sweet-spot intervals
RPE 7: hard but holdable, a few words at a time. This is the biggest fitness driver for century riding. 5 min easy between reps. Progression: Wk 1–3 = 3 × 12 min · Wk 4–6 = 3 × 15 min · Wk 7–8 = 2 × 20 min · Wk 9–10 = 3 × 20 min.
2–3 sets 12–20 min RPE 7
M2
Over-unders (add from Wk 6)
Alternate 1 min @ RPE 7 / 1 min @ RPE 8 inside each rep. Teaches you to ride through a surge and recover while still pedalling, exactly what rolling terrain demands. 4 min easy between sets.
3 × 8 min RPE 7↔8

ATHLEEKCycling Day 3 · Endurance · 2–5.5 hr
Main Ride · Endurance
M1
Steady endurance ride
RPE 3–4: you should be able to hold a full conversation the whole way. If you can't talk in sentences, you're going too hard. Watch HR drift late in long rides; ease off rather than let it climb. Time in the saddle wins the century, not speed.
progressive RPE 3–4 85–95 rpm
M2
Tempo surges (from Wk 5)
RPE 5–6 on the climbs to mimic the rolling parts of the route. Eat just before each surge so you rehearse fuelling under load.
4–6 × 5 min RPE 5–6
